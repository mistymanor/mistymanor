Riding Lessons

At Misty Manor we offer lessons in Western riding year round. We have certified instructors to help you achieve your riding goals while having loads of fun.

Additionally Cowboy Camp is offered in the summer. Cowboy Camp is a way and a time for you and your family to learn about horses from the ground up. It is like owning your own horse for a week. Campers learn how to brush, feed, tack, and, of course, ride the horse. Of the 6 hours of camp each day, typically campers are on the horse for 4 of those hours. We'd love to tell you more, please give us a call!

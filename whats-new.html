Cowboy Camp 2025

Misty Manor is proud to announce registration is open for Cowboy Camp!

Do your kids love horse? Send them to Cowboy Camp! We have camp weeks in June, July and August and information packets are available! To have a packet emailed to you, please call the ranch: 443-277-4693.

Ride to Lunch

Come to lunch at the Woodstock Inn with Misty Manor! We make a full day of it, leaving the ranch by 10a, eating at the restaurant, and riding back by 3p. We cross the river twice each way!

Spring rides are April 27, May 18 and June 1. April's ride is full but we still have room in May and June.



To learn more, call: (443) 277-4693
